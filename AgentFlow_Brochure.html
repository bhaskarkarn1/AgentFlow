01 — The Problem
Enterprise workflows are broken.
Three high-frequency workflows silently drain thousands of hours and lakhs of rupees every year in mid-size enterprises. They involve multiple teams, systems, and endless manual coordination.
👤
Employee Onboarding
When a new employee joins, HR must manually create accounts across 4–6 systems (Workday, JIRA, Slack, Calendar, Email, ERP), assign a buddy, schedule orientations, and verify everything — involving 3–5 people across departments.
⏱ 4–6 hours per hire × 200 hires/year = 1,000+ hours wasted
📋
Meeting-to-Action Pipeline
After every team meeting, someone must manually extract action items, assign owners, create JIRA tasks, and send follow-ups. 40% of action items are forgotten within 48 hours (Harvard Business Review).
📉 40% of action items lost · 45 min manual follow-up per meeting
⚡
SLA Breach Prevention
When a procurement approver goes on leave, approvals sit stuck for 24–48 hours. The SLA clock keeps ticking. Someone must find a delegate, reroute the approval, and document the override.
💸 ₹5,00,000/day in SLA penalties while approvals sit idle
Total annual cost of these broken workflows
₹83+ Lakhs
per year, for a 500-employee company

02 — The Solution
8 AI agents. One mission.
AgentFlow replaces manual coordination with specialized AI agents. Each has one job. Data flows through the pipeline. If something fails, the system heals itself. If it matters, it asks you first.
1
📡
Signal Ingestor
Takes in the request. Extracts entities. Classifies urgency. Runs security scans against 20+ injection patterns.
2
🔍
Root Cause Analyzer
Queries enterprise systems to diagnose what's wrong. Detects cross-system conflicts, ghost entities, and data inconsistencies.
3
📐
Strategy Planner
Creates multi-step execution plans with tool calls, owner assignments, and dependency ordering. Validates against hallucinated tools.
4
🎓
GPA Evaluator
Scores plan quality on 0.0–4.0 GPA scale. Detects contradictory reasoning. Plans below 2.0 are sent back for revision.

Execution Engine

7

Routes unrecoverable failures to the right human authority. Detects circular delegation loops and authority gaps.

03 — What Makes It Different
Not a chatbot. An operations team.
Most AI tools answer questions. AgentFlow takes multi-step actions across real systems, handles failures, and maintains a complete audit trail.
🔧
Self-Healing Execution
When a tool fails — API timeout, 403, rate limit — the system retries with exponential backoff, credential rotation, and graceful degradation. The dashboard shows exact error codes and recovery actions.
👁️
Full Transparency
Every agent decision, tool call, and API response is logged live. A persistent SQLite database with 9 tables records every action for compliance review. No black box.
🧑
Human-in-the-Loop (HITL)
Before critical actions, the system pauses with a quality score. You approve, reject, or ask questions. 5-min timeout auto-escalates to supervisor — nothing stalls.
🛡️
51+ Edge Cases Handled
A centralized Edge Case Engine monitors 11 categories: duplicate identities, circular delegation, ghost entities, prompt injection, race conditions, and more.
